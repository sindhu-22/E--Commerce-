EcoShop Sales Performance and Growth Recovery


Project Overview

This project analyzes EcoShop's historical sales data to identify revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, 
and the impact of discounts on profitability. A sales forecasting model using Prophet predicts future sales, 
while an interactive Power BI dashboard provides business insights to support growth and improve decision-making.

 Business Problem

EcoShop has experienced a plateau in quarter-over-quarter revenue. The goal is to identify underperforming 
product categories, evaluate discount effectiveness, and improve the overall 
profit margin from 12% to 15% through data-driven analysis.

 Objectives

Analyze sales performance and revenue trends.
Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories.
Evaluate customer spending behavior.
Measure discount impact on profitability.
Forecast future sales using Prophet.
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
